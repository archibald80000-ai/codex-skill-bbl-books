# Golden Sample Notes

Golden sample:

`项目资料打印版手册_黄金样例.docx`

The first stable version should preserve this direction:

- Simple formal cover.
- Clean standalone table of contents.
- No internal processing logs in the formal document.
- Clear chapter structure.
- Each chapter has a short explanation plus a table or checklist.
- Keep parameters, steps, delivery requirements, and compliance details.
- Remove AI dialogue filler.
- Do not write cleaning process into the formal document.
- Suitable for direct printing.
- Images are handled as an album chapter.
- No original image filename, path, size, width, height, or orientation in captions.

Do not regress to:

- Concatenating source files by filename.
- Putting cleaning reports into the body.
- Putting similarity analysis into the body.
- Showing file tables, dimensions, or errors in the body.
